Basic Mashed Potatoes

Description

This mashed potato recipe is perfect if you're looking for good old fasioned mashed potatoes. Made with just potatoes, milk, butter, and garlic, this go-to recipe makes a classic holiday dish.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes
  2. Heat the milk
  3. Mash the potatoes
